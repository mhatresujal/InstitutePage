About Us

Established in 1887 as Victoria Jubilee Technical Institute, VJTI Mumbai has pioneered India’s Engineering education, research and training ecosystem and has been shifting itself from a training institute to capacity building institute with focus on research, innovation, and technology for society. Later, its name was changed to Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute.VJTI is also the Central Technical Institute of Maharashtra State. The institute trains students in engineering and technology at the certificate,diploma, degree, post-graduate and doctoral levels.

Our Courses

  • Undergraduate (UG) Courses: VJTI offers B.Tech programs in nine specializations. These include fields such as Civil Engineering,Computer Engineering,Electrical Engineering,Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, information Technology,Mechanical Engineering,Production Engineering,Textile Engineering and Chemical Engineering.

  • Postgraduate (PG) Courses:
    • M.Tech: VJTI provides M.Tech programs in nine specializations. Students can pursue advanced studies in areas like Structural Engineering, Electronics, and Communication Engineering, etc.
    • MCA (Master of Computer Applications): For those interested in computer applications, VJTI offers a Master’s program in MCA.

  • Diploma Courses: VJTI also offers diploma courses for students. These programs cover various technical disciplines.

  • Ph.D. Programs: VJTI provides opportunities for research and doctoral studies through its Ph.D. programs.
